🥐Croissant

A croissant is a French pastry made from a laminated yeast dough, which gives it a flaky and buttery texture. It is inspired by the Austrian kipferl and is known for its crescent shape. Croissants are typically light, airy, and have a deeply caramelized buttery flavor, making them a popular choice for breakfast or as a snack. If you're interested in making croissants, there are many recipes available that guide you through the process of creating this delicious pastry.
